QueueIT Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

QueueIT ("we", "our", or "the app") is a party queue app that lets you add songs and vote on what plays next. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Account & profile. When you sign up (email, Apple, or Google), we store your user ID, email (if provided), and a chosen username. App Clip guests may sign in anonymously—we only store an anonymized identifier.

Music preferences. We store your music provider choice (Apple Music, Spotify, or none) and, if you use Spotify, an encrypted refresh token to search for songs. We store your Apple Music storefront (e.g. country) to show the correct catalog.

Session & queue data. We store session join codes, songs you add to the queue, and votes—tied to your user ID so we can run the queue correctly. Other participants in a session can see usernames and song choices.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate the app: running sessions, managing queues, resolving songs between Spotify and Apple Music, and authenticating you. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (hosted infrastructure)
  • Apple Music (MusicKit) — song search and playback when you connect Apple Music
  • Spotify — song search and catalog when you connect Spotify

These services have their own privacy policies. We may use optional error-tracking services (e.g. Sentry) to fix bugs; such data is anonymized and used only for diagnostics.

Data Retention

We retain your account and session data for as long as your account exists. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Contact).

Your Rights

You can request access to your data, correction of errors, or deletion of your account. Contact us at support@queueitapp.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the revised policy here and note the date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests: support@queueitapp.com
