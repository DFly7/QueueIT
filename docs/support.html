Support

Get help with QueueIT.

How can we help?

For bugs, feature requests, or questions about your account, reach out by email. We typically respond within a few business days.

Email: support@queueitapp.com

Common questions

I can't join a session. Make sure you're using the correct join code or link. Check your internet connection and try again.

My song didn't appear / won't play. QueueIT matches songs between Spotify and Apple Music. If the host uses a different provider, some songs may not be available in their catalog.

I want to delete my account. Email us at support@queueitapp.com with the email you used to sign up. We'll confirm and remove your data.
